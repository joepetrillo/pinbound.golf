## Three steps

1. Connect your tee sheet provider.
2. Review the greeting, rates, and escalation rules.
3. Forward the phone line whenever you're ready.

## Start small if you want

Plenty of courses begin with after-hours only, then expand to overflow during busy stretches once they've heard it in action.
