## The 9pm booking window

Evening callers usually know what they want: a time, a player count, and a quick confirmation. That's a two-minute call that would otherwise go to voicemail.

## Handled by policy, not improvisation

After-hours answers follow the same rules the shop uses during the day, so the morning staff never finds a surprise on the tee sheet.
