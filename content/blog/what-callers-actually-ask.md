Placeholder post for layout testing. The pro shop phone isn't unpredictable. Most calls are one of five things: booking a time, checking availability, asking about rates, asking about conditions, or something that genuinely needs a person.

## The five buckets

Bookings and availability checks alone make up the bulk of call volume, and both have clear answers sitting in the tee sheet.

## The fifth bucket matters most

The calls that don't fit a bucket are exactly the ones your staff should spend time on. Automation earns its keep by clearing the other four out of the way.
