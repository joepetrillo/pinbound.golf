## Bursts, not a steady stream

Weather calls arrive all at once, right when the staff is busiest assessing the course. Answering the same question forty times is exactly the work worth automating.

## One policy, every caller

Whether it's a frost delay or cart-path-only, every caller hears the same answer the shop decided on—no drift between call one and call forty.
