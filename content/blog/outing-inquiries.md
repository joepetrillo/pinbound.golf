Placeholder post for layout testing. A corporate outing inquiry can be worth more than a week of regular tee times, and it often arrives as a casual phone call.

## High value, low urgency

Outing planners rarely call twice. If the first call lands in voicemail, the event usually lands at another course.

## Capture, then escalate

Pinbound gathers the essentials—group size, preferred dates, contact info—and routes the inquiry straight to the person who books events.
