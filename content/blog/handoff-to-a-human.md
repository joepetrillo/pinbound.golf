Placeholder post for layout testing. Some calls should always reach a person: a complaint, an outing inquiry worth thousands of dollars, or a caller who simply asks for one.

## Clear escalation rules

Pinbound hands off the moment a call goes beyond its policy—no wandering, no improvised answers about things it doesn't know.

## What the staff sees

Every handoff arrives with context: who's calling, what they asked for, and what's already been confirmed, so nobody has to start the conversation over.
