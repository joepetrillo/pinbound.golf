Placeholder post for layout testing. Pinbound doesn't keep a separate calendar—it reads and writes the tee sheet your course already runs on.

## One source of truth

When a caller books a 9:40 for four players, the reservation shows up exactly where a staff member would have put it. No sync delays, no duplicate systems.

## What happens when the sheet is full

If the requested time isn't available, Pinbound offers the nearest open slots instead of guessing or overbooking.
